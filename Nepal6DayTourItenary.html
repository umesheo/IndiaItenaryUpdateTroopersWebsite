Nepal 6 Day Tour

About The Place

Nepal is a small, landlocked country, situated between Tibet (China) to its north, and India to its east, west and south. At latitudes between 26 and 30 degrees north and longitudes between 80 and 88 degrees east, the country covers an area of 147,181sq.km and stretches approximately 145-241 km.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 -✈ Kathmandu

    Hotel Flight
    • Fly over the world's highest peak, Mount Everest at an altitude of 8848M, and arrive at Kathmandu, a central valley in Nepal.
    • It is a city where old and new buildings coexist, just like Europe in the Middle Ages, old but not heavy.
    • The local folk customs are simple, surrounded by lakes and mountains, and the scenery is exceptionally beautiful. Because it is located on the southern slope of the Himalayas, this natural barrier shields the city from the cold wind from the north.
    • The southern part of the city faces the warm current of the Indian Ocean.
    • The unique geographical environment makes the annual average temperature around 20℃.
    • The climate is pleasant, sunny and green all year round. With lush trees and blooming flowers, it has the reputation of "paradise in the mountains" and is a world-famous tourist attraction. (According to the actual arrival time of the flight, arrange whether to go to the scenic spot).

Upon arrival, visit the unfathomable Devi's Fall (visit time 40 minutes).
    • David Falls is about 4 kilometers from Pokhara in the Kingdom of Nepal. The wonderful thing about this waterfall is that underneath it is an underground cavern.

There is a lot of heat in the cave all year round, and you can feel the obvious heat with your hands, and the water vapor will change into colorful colors in the air.     Hotel
    • If the weather is good, the guide will wake you up at about 4 in the morning, and then drive to Sarangkot, which is located on a hill on the edge of the Pokhara Valley, where you can watch the Himalayas sunrise very closely (time 100 minutes), looking at the Annapurna peaks of the Himalayas, the stunning sunrise and changing colors, the 10,000-year-old ice and snow giant peaks, and the fishtail peaks are golden, which is particularly spectacular. After that, return to the hotel for breakfast.
    • After breakfast, go boating on the beautiful Fewa Lake (about 1 hour) and admire the beautiful lakes and mountains of Pokhara Valley. Visit the Shakti Temple (Barali) in the lake.

    • It is known as "Medieval Nepal "The essence and treasure house of art", known as the "open-air museum", shows a magnificent picture of medieval Nepal.
