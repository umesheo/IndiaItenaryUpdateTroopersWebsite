Golden Triangle Tour

About The Tour

The Golden Triangle Tour is the perfect blend of an architectural masterpiece and opulent culture of Kathmandu valley, fabulous wildlife activities of Chitwan and spectacular adventurous activities as well as miraculous natural scenery of Pokhara. You will start your tour visiting the UNESCO listed world heritage sites of Kathmandu, exploring the age-old architecture and miraculous traditions. A visit to Chitwan means unlimited fun and lively activities with wildlife. While you are in Pokhara, you will sojourn on the ecastic natural scenery and pay a visit to world famous lakes and temples.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 - Arrival in Kathmandu

    Guest House
    • Arrival at Kathmandu(TIA).
    • Welcome to the land of Himalayas! Your enduring journey in the historical masterpiece would start with a warm welcome and sincere hospitality of one of the members from a star hotel. S/he will pick you up from the airport and help you check in one of the luxurious star hotels of Kathmandu.
    • In the afternoon, have some rest in the gratifying aura or stroll around the tourism hub of Kathmandu. Make sure all your dues are cleared and you have all the necessary documents, or at least inform us in case you need any support to avoid complications during the travel.
    • We will then brief you about the travel guidelines including the vital preparations for the trip, insurance policies and fix up an introduction to travel guides. Please pre-inform us if you will be arriving late and you can’t attend the pre-trip meeting.
    • In the evening, you will have a great taste of Nepalese food in one of the authentic Nepalese cuisines.

  • Day 2 - Sightseeing of Kathmandu

    Guest House
    • Let’s start on the first day of your wonderful vacation in Nepal visiting the world-famous heritage sites and brilliant architectures of Kathmandu valley after having breakfast. You will visit the following sites with a professional tour guide in a private vehicle:
    • 1. Kathmandu Durbar Square
    • 2. Pashupatinath Temple
    • 3. Swyambhunath Temple
    • 4. Bouddhanath Stupa
    • 5. Patan Durbar Square
    • 6. Bhaktapur Durbar Square

  • Day 3 - Drive from Kathmandu to Chitwan

    1 hours Guest House 175km
    • After breakfast, we will leave for Chitwan via Prithvi Highway along the banks of Trishuli river, relishing in the view of green terrace lands on both sides of the river. We may spare a few hours for rafting in Trishuli river if time allows.
    • You will snap through Mugling, where the two rivers Trishuli and Marsyangdi meets. The trip will pass through vivacious rivers and streams until you reach the East-West highway. It will take around 30 minutes drive through East-west highway to reach Sauraha, Chitwan.
    • You will get a superb view of the sunset from the Rapti river and enjoy the awe-inspiring Tharu culture. Dinner will be followed by a trip to the Tharu villages either on foot or tonka.

  • Day 4 - Full Day Jungle Safari Activities and Exploration

    Guest House
    • During jungle safari, you will get a visit to Ghariyal Crocodile Breeding Center passing through wondrous natural lakes inside the National Park. You will pass by the crash of one-horned rhino several times as the excursion route is designed accordingly. A herd of various species of deer, elephants and wild water buffalo are frequently seen. Beautiful birds, different reptiles and animals grazing in the yard of army camp would also provide us with unlimited fun.
    • Either chill in the bank taking a sip of your favourite drink or get on the back of the elephant to dive in the magnificent Rapti river while it is bathing. You will also visit the Elephant Breeding Centre where you can see baby elephants and give a shot to the museum where you can see elephant bones, riding equipment, posters and many more.
    • You will get a wonderful view of endangered birds from Meghauli, as it houses around 600 species of birds. Some of them are temporary migrants here to adapt themselves in the changing climatic conditions.
    • You will be visiting the Tharu Cultural Museum and Research Centre and join the Tharu Cultural Program and Stick Dance to discover indigenous Tharu Culture.

  • Day 5 - Drive from Chitwan to Pokhara

    Guest House
    • After breakfast, we will travel to Pokhara via Narayanghadh-Mugling road and cross the bridge over Trishuli River and enter into Tanahu District. We will follow the highway along Marsyandi River. You can see the landscapes slowing changing as we move ahead.
    • We will pass through Marsyandi Hydropower powerhouse and cross the places like Dumre, Damauli until we reach to Pokhara. After checking in Hotel, we will visit a deadly Davis Fall locally known as Patale Chango(Hell's Fall), that claimed the life of Swiss tourist Davis and was named after him.
    • Then we move to Phewa Lake, the charm of Pokhara where we can enjoy boating and sailing across the lake and visiting island temple called Talhbarahi. Walking on the shorelines of Fewa Lake with lake gentle lake bridge and reflection of Fishtail over spectacular water is incredible here.

  • Day 6 - Pokhara Sightseeing

    Guest House 3.5 hours
    • Shree Bindhyabasini Temple sight seeing.
    • Devi's Fall sight seeing.
    • Devi's Fall sight seeing.
    • Gupteswar Gupha seeing.
    • Gupteswar Gupha seeing.
    • Phewa Tal (Fewa Lake) seeing.
    • Seti River Gorge sight seeing.

  • Day 6 - : Sarangkot Hill Tour and Kathmandu Drive

    Guest House 200km 5 hours
    • Early morning before dawn, we will depart to Sarankot hill to catch the glimpse of Sunrise from Annapurna Mountain Range along with the panoramic view of Fishtail, Dhaulagiri, Manaslu and other snow-capped mountains
    • From Sarankot we can see entire Pokhara valley. On the return way to Hotel, we can visit Mahendra cave, Bat cave and Barahi Temple
    • If time allows, short drive to Begnas Lake and Rupa lake and visit to World Peace Pagoda would add fun to your Pokhara visit. If you take a bus, it would take 5-6 hrs drive to reach Kathmandu or we can choose 30 minutes flight from Pokhara to Kathmandu.
