Upper Mustang

About The Place

Upper Mustang (formerly Kingdom of Lo) is an upper part (Northern area) of Mustang District. The Upper Mustang was a restricted kingdom and demilitarized area until 1992 which makes it one of the most preserved regions in the world, with a majority of the population still speaking traditional Tibetic languages.
