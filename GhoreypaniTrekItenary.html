Ghorepani

About The Place

Ghorepani is a village in Myagdi District in the Dhaulagiri Zone of northern-central Nepal. It lies 17 kilometres from the district capital of Beni at an elevation of approximately 2874 m (9429 ft).Ghorepani lies on a major trail linking several other villages as well as the Annapurna Base Camp.
