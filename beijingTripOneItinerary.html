Beijing Trip One

About The Place

Beijing Is China's Ancient AND Modern Capital. For more than 800 years, Beijing served as the capital of six ancient dynasties, the most famous being the Ming (1368–1644) and Qing (1644–1912) dynasties. It is home to the greatest collection of sites from China's imperial past, including the Great Wall of China, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 - Arrival at Beijing

    Hotel
    • Take a domestic flight to Beijing today. Upon arrival, someone will pick you up and take you to the hotel to rest.In the evening, go to one of the oldest streets in Beijing [Nanluoguxiang] to experience the hutong culture and find specialties.
    • Check in to the hotel to rest, and free activities.
    Reminder:
    1. Please take care of your belongings while admiring the ancient charm of Nanluoguxiang.

  • Day 2 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • In the morning, you will visit the Imperial Palace of Ming and Qing Dynasties in China [The Palace Museum], and overlook the Forbidden City from the commanding height of the city 【Jingshan Park】.
    • You will visit [Shichahai]Beijing's best-preserved place, and then go to 【Deyunshe】 to listen to an authentic cross talk in the afternoon.
    • Then return to the hotel to rest
    Reminder:
    1. Due to the large area of ​​the Palace Museum and the newly opened many areas, the walking time is longer. It is recommended to wear tourist shoes or thick-soled shoes, and bring water and dry food!

  • Day 3 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, you will climb the "Juyongguan Great Wall", which is the "No.1 Pass in the World".
    • You will go to 【Ming MingTombs】 one of the largest existing imperial tomb complexes in the country with the largest number of emperors’ tombs, and learn about the last memories left by ancient emperors.
    • Return to the hotel to rest after the tour
    Reminders:
    1. Wear sports non-slip shoes in advance and pay attention to safety when climbing the Great Wall!
    2. The mausoleum is designated as a non- smoking area and all fireworks are strictly prohibited. It is strictly forbidden to paint on trees, historical sites and public facilities, and it is strictly forbidden to climb mausoleum walls, fences, Minglou and other buildings.

  • Day 4 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • You will go to the world's topuniversity [Tsinghua University] to experience the influence of knowledge in the morning.
    • Afterwards, you will visit [Old Summer Palace] to pay tribute to the wreckage, looking for the glorious empire.
    • At noon you can choose to have a meal nearby in Shichahai
    • In the afternoon, you will visit the "Royal Garden Museum" [Summer Palace] and stroll along the shore of Kunming Lake and the foot of Longevity Mountain.
    • Return to the hotel to rest
    Reminder:
    1. Due to the large area of ​​the Summer Palace and long walking time, it is recommended to wear tourist shoes or thick-soled shoes, and bring water and dry food!

  • Day 5 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • In the morning, you will visit the National Museum to visit the cultural heritage of China for thousands of years.
    • You will experience the life of old Beijing and taste special cuisine [Dashilan].
    • In the evening, night tour of China’s most beautiful and dangerous [Simatai Great Wall] is organized for you.
    • Return to the hotel to rest
    Warm Reminder:
    1. Night tour of Simatai Great Wall. The western end of the ancient town is connected to the Simatai Great Wall scenic spot. Be sure to pay attention to safety when you visit at night.

  • Day 6 - Explore Beijing (Last Day)

    • After having breakfast, you will take a slow tour in the majesty of the ancient northern towns, and there are also many [Gubei Water Towns] with the characteristics of Jiangnan water towns.
    • In the afternoon, a special person will drop you off to the airport.
    • End a pleasant trip to Beijing.
