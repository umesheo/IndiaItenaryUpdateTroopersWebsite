Hangzou to Beijing

About The Place

Beijing Is China's Ancient And Modern Capital. For more than 800 years, Beijing served as the capital of six ancient dynasties, the most famous being the Ming (1368–1644) and Qing (1644–1912) dynasties. It is home to the greatest collection of sites from China's imperial past, including the Great Wall of China, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 - Hangzou To Beijing

    2h 15m / 21h 34m / 5h 29m
    Hotel 1,286 km
    • Tourists choose their own means of transportation whether by plane (2h 15min), bus (21h 34m), train (5h 29m) or private vehicle to arrive at the capital of the motherland-Beijing.
    • Professional service personnel will pick you up the station.
    • Check in to the hotel to rest, and free activities.
    Reminders:
    1. This day only includes pick-up car + accommodation,others are not included.
    2. The northern climate is dry, it is recommended to drink plenty of water and pay attention to skin care.

  • Day 2 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • In the morning, go to visit [Tiananmen Square], visit Tiananmen Square, the Monument to the People’s Heroes, the exterior view of the Great Hall of the People, visit [Chairman Mao Memorial Hall] (closed on Monday, visit the exterior view in case of government shutdown)
    • Then, proceed to visit [Forbidden City Museum], Feel the grand mind of the Prosperous Dynasty from the ups and downs of the Forbidden City's architectural movements
    • After leaving the Forbidden City, you can overlook the whole view of the Imperial City in Jingshan Park
    • In the evening at [Lao She Tea House], enjoy the wonderful performances of opera and drama celebrities full of Beijing flavor
    • Then return to the hotel to rest
    Reminders:
    1. It is not allowed to bring handbags, backpacks, cameras, video cameras,drinking glasses, drinks and other items into the venue when visiting the Mao Zedong Memorial Hall. Friends with these items should deposit them in the depository. A certain fee is required for storage, and the number of cameras is calculated.
    2. The Palace Museum has strict security checks, so please do not bring lighters. 3. It takes a long time to walk today. It is recommended to wear tourist shoes or thick- soled shoes, and bring water and dry food!

  • Day 3 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • In the morning (it is recommended to leave at around 8 o'clock to avoid traffic jams), drive to the [Mutianyu Great Wall] in Huairou District, Beijing
    • It is an important part of the Great Wall of China, a great defense project in ancient China, and one of the passes of the Ming Great Wall
    • In the afternoon, we will return to downtown Beijing t visit the [Bird's Nest] [Water Cube] location.
    • Return to the hotel to rest after the tour
    Reminders:
    1. There are three ways to goup and down the mountainto visit the Mutianyu Great Wall: 1. Cable car; 2. Ropeway; 3. Climbing the Great Wall on foot. You can choose by yourself.
    2. There are many steps o the Great Wall. Please pay attention to safety at your feet. Do not walk when taking pictures or take pictures when walking.

  • Day 4 - Explore Beijing

    Hotel
    • In the morning, you will visit the [Garden of Prince Gong’s Mansion]. It was once the residences of Heshen and Yonglin. It has gone through the historical process of the Qing Dynasty from its heyday to its decline. It carries extremely rich historical and cultural information. Therefore, there is "a mansion of Prince Gong"., Half of Qing Dynasty history"
    • Afterwards, take a tricycle to stroll around the hutongs and feel the local customs of the old Beijing hutongs.
    • At noon you can choose to have a meal nearby in Shichahai
    • -In the afternoon, go to [Nanluoguxiang] to visit, and then go to [Qianmen Dashilan] to visit the old Beijing Jing commercial district, feel the style of the old Beijing in the 1920s and 1930s, with the appearance of century-old shops
    • Return to the hotel to rest
    Reminders:
    1. It is possible to arrange a departure time with the driver for the travel time of the day.
    2. The cost of hutong tricycles varies. The implementation of the bargaining system is recommended under the advice of the tour guide. The cost generally ranges from 50-100 per person, subject to the actual price of the tricycle owner!
    3. After the tour guide in the Dashilan area of ​​Qianme takes a period of time, the rest is basically the free time for guests, and the gathering time can be agreed with the tour guide.
    4. Food recommendation: Quanjude Roast Duck Restaurant (reservation required), Duyi Shaomai Restaurant, Yueshengzhai, etc.

  • Day 5 - Beijing to Hangzou

    2h 15m / 21h 34m / 5h 29m
    1,286 km
    • After you wake up, you will be sent to the airport or high- speed rail station by private car according to the return time to end your trip to Beijing!
