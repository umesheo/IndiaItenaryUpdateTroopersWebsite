Yinchuan

About The Place

Yinchuan is the capital city of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, with the Yellow River passing through the border to the east and Helan Mountain to the west. It is known as the "Stop the Jiangnan and the Land of Fish and Rice". In 2018, it was awarded the title of the first batch of "International Wetland Cities" in the world. It is not the south of the Yangtze River that it is better than the south of the Yangtze River. The little lights of the city are woven into a dazzling golden dress. Under the night of Yinchuan, the light and shadow blend and the mountains and rivers reflect each other. It is a colorful landscape, and the picturesque night view of Lake City is intoxicating.
