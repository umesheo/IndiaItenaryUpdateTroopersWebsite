Harbin

About The Place

Harbin, with a total land area of 53,068 km2 (20,490 sq mi), is located in southern Heilongjiang province and is the provincial capital. The prefecture is also located at the southeastern edge of the Songnen Plain, a major part of China's Northeastern Plain

Itinerary

  • Day 1 - Harbin

    Hotel
    • Today, arrive in the ice city of Harbin by plane where you will be picked up by our tour expert by a car and you will be taken to the hotel for a rest.In the afternoon, under the leadership of a local guide, go to the St. Sophia Church to visit. This building is known as the most perfect Byzantine building and is also the Harbin Architecture Art Museum.
    • Then you will stroll to Central Street, passing by the Flood Control Memorial Tower and the Riverside Railway Bridge, where you can taste snacks such as ginger cola, madele popsicles, candied haws and madele sausages, or taste the must-eat “Laochang spring cakes” "Northeastern dishes, steamed pork wrapped in pot, shredded pork with Beijing sauce, etc., will provide energy for tonight's event.
    • In the evening: you will come to Harbin Ice and Snow World, where you can enjoy beautiful ice sculptures, snow sculptures and ice castles. As night falls, the feast of ice lanterns rendered by various lights is even more fascinating. At the same time, you can experience ice slides, ice mazes and ice Tug of war and other activities.
    Reminder:
    1. The temperature difference between indoor and outdoor in Harbin is generally around 50°C in winter. Down jacket, cotton pants, snow boots, hats, gloves, collars, sunglasses, water cups, sunscreen, frostbite cream, and masks are all necessary. Indispensable.
    2. Ice and Snow World is expected to open around December 20 every year, and the park is expected to close around February 23. The exact date is subject to the official announcement. It is mainly affected by temperature and is not controlled by humans.

  • Day 2 - Harbin to Yabuli

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, you will take the "Snow Country Train" to Yabuli Ski Resort, dress up and wear snow clothes upon arrival.
    • Then take the ancient local transportation to ride on the ice and snow. Accompanied by the babbling sound~ It’s really interesting! In the afternoon, go to the Yabuli Ski Resort Ski Center to go through the skiing procedures. You can choose the beginner/intermediate/advanced road skiing according to your needs and enjoy skiing.
    • In the evening, experience the snow hot spring, enjoy the warm winter, and get rid of fatigue.
    Reminder:
    1. Skiing is a high-risk sports activity. Please pay attention to your personal safety. If you cannot participate in skiing for your own reasons, you can replace it with [Yabuli Mountain Sightseeing Ropeway], please inform in advance.

  • Day 3 - Yabuli to Xuexiang

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, head to the fairy tale kingdom---China Xuexiang (Shuangfeng Forest Farm), you will ride a snowmobile through the virgin forest, melt into the vast snow, and visit the location of "Where are you going to dad".
    • You can wear national costumes and leather jackets, stroll the Northeast New Year Street, or interact with the villagers to experience window-cutting, grinding tofu, big pipes, candied haws...Looking for the lost traditional folk customs of the Northeast.
    • Then, transfer to the station bus and enter the Xuexiang Scenic Area, where there are the mountains of the North, the densest forest and the sea, the thickest snow, the cleanest and transparent sunshine, and the simplest life of a lumberjack. Strolling around Xueyun Street, walking on the only main street in Xuexiang, wearing thick clothes, wearing gloves and hats, we have snowball fights together, making snowmen, or drinking hot milk tea and enjoying the snow quietly.
    • In the evening. experience the wonderful performance of the Northeast people "Ningshe a meal, reluctantly turn for two".

  • Day 4 - Xuexiang

    Hotel
    • Enjoy yourself in the morning with a cup of hot milk in winter, experience the local dog sledding activities under the guidance of the guide, and swim in Xueyun Street; the "flying saucer" on the ice and snow is definitely ski ring, experience the speed of ice and snow on the slide.
    • In the afternoon, come to the Fairytale Post Office at 44°north latitude, buy a postcard, personally write the good wishes, put it in the exclusive delivery wooden box, and send the best wishes to family and friends.
    • Then follow the guide to the Xuexiang Homestay Museum, which recorded the development and changes of Xuexiang. There are more than 20 shapes of snowflakes.
    Reminder:
    1. The activity experience in Xuexiang can be adjusted according to the actual rhythm

  • Day 5 - Xuexiang to Harbin

    Hotel
    • In the morning, the golden sunlight is shining on the white snow, flashing with dazzling light. At this time, we come to Dream Homeland, board the sightseeing platform, Xuexiang where the scenery is unobstructed, and the gorgeousness cannot be taken away with the camera.
    • Climbing up the mountainside observation deck along the wooden plank road, you can take a panoramic view of the manor and Xuexiang, one by one, the naive snow mushrooms, and the red lanterns.
    • In our Northeast, there is nothing that can't be solved by iron pot stew. Looking at the snow outside the window, eating steaming iron pot stew in the house at noon.
    • You will bid farewell to Xuexiang and return to Harbin in the afternoon.

  • Day 6 - Harbin

    • In the morning, you will visit the Lao Daowai·Chinese Baroque Historical and Cultural District where you need to slow down and listen carefully, watch attentively. Here you can watch the most complete preserved In the Chinese Baroque building complex. You can also taste the century-old restaurant Lao Dingfeng dim sum and other delicacies.
    • In the afternoon, if reading is also a kind of travel, then studying at Gogol Bookstore is to travel in a beautiful world. Wooden handrails, stained glass windows, and European-style domes greet you, as if you are traveling through it. You can post a postcard to preserve this beautiful memory.
    • End a pleasant trip to Beijing.
