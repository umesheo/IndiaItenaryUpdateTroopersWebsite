Beijing Trip Five

About The Place

Beijing Is China's Ancient And Modern Capital. For more than 800 years, Beijing served as the capital of six ancient dynasties, the most famous being the Ming (1368–1644) and Qing (1644–1912) dynasties. It is home to the greatest collection of sites from China's imperial past, including the Great Wall of China, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 - Flight to Xining


    Hotel
    • Qinghai has too many dream scenes, golden flowers, dazzling snow-capped mountains, and sea-like clear water Suddenly, I thought I was in a dream.
    • Xining is 2200 meters above sea level. You can also rest in the hotel to recover from the exhaustion on the way down.
    • Xining has a dry climate. Avoid unrestrained consumption of mutton. You should also consider the problem of unacceptable water and soil. You can bring synthetic vitamins such as Jinshierkang and Shancun tablets to supplement your vitamin intake daily to ensure your fitness.

  • Day 2 - Visit the birthplace of the second Buddha "Tsongkhapa"

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, drive to (approximately 35 kilometers/45 minutes) to visit the birthplace of the second Buddha "Tsongkhapa" in the world-[Ta'er Monastery] to appreciate the three wonders of art: butter flowers, murals, pile embroidery.
    • After that, take a bus to Qinghai Lake (150km/about 2 hours), and arrive at the long-awaited Qinghai Lake Erlangjian Scenic Area. Upon arrival, enjoy lunch near the scenic area.Visit the Pearl of the Plateau-Qinghai Lake Erlangjian Scenic Are.
    • In the afternoon, you can freely move around in the scenic area, and you can stroll along the Qinghai Lake and feel the beauty of the plateau lake.
    • In the evening, stay in the Heima River, have dinner, and then go to the lake to take pictures of the sunset by the Qinghai Lake.
    Reminders:
    1. It is not allowed to take pictures in the Ta'er Temple. Please follow the instructions and safety tips of the on-site staff and visit in order.
    2. Today, I have to climb over 3500 meters above sea level Laji Mountain, pay attention to slow down activities, do not exercise vigorously; drink plenty of water, eat more fruits, eat seventy percent full for meals, increase the amount of sunscreen to prevent ultraviolet sunburn.
    3. You can take pictures on the edge of Qinghai Lake, and you can see rape blossoms in July. There are fixed parking spots for parking, but you can't park elsewhere. In summer, there is a lot of traffic and the traffic police will not allow you to stop casually.

  • Day 3 - Dachaidan

    Hotel
    • You can wake up at 6 o'clock in the morning to watch the sunrise on Qinghai Lake. After having breakfast, drive to the beautiful Chaka Salt Lake (80 km/about 1 hour). On the way, you will climb the Rubber Mountain (3817 meters above sea level) and arrive at an important salt producing area in China. Caka Salt Lake (tour about 1.5 hours). Qaidam is known as the "salt world". The Chaka Salt Lake is one of the four largest salt lakes in the Qaidam Basin and is a famous crystallization lake.
    • In the afternoon, drive to Dachaidan in Delingha (about 4 hours).
    Reminders:
    1. When visiting Chaka Salt Lake, you must follow the instructions and safety tips of the on-site staff, take care of the environment and pay attention to hygiene, leaving only the back and the background photos in the camera.
    2. Matters needing attention: The ultraviolet rays of Chaka Salt Lake are strong, so you must be good at sun protection, wear sunglasses, and pay attention to the small black holes in the lake.
    3. Wear with: brightly colored clothes to shoot better results mirror the sky, in addition to the well-known red skirt, you can also choose choose bright yellow, bright blue, or grape purple and other bright colors are very beautiful.

  • Day 4 -Drive to Water Yadan

    Hotel
    • -In the morning, drive to Water Yadan, which is the largest, most mature, best image and most representative Yadan horseshoe crab community discovered in the world so far. This wind-eroded mound hidden deep in the hinterland of the desert, like a mysterious old castle, stands on the blue-gray Gobi, showing a bright yellow against the blue sky and white clouds.
    • After driving along the straight Gobi highway to the ancient city of Dunhuang, after passing through Dangjin Mountain and the oil town [Axai Petroleum Town] where "Nine-Layered Demon Tower" was filmed , continue on the road and rush to Dunhuang to check in and rest.
    Reminders:
    1. The journey is long today, around 9 hours, it is recommended to bring dry food and water in advance to supplement energy.

  • Day 5 - Mingsha Mountain

    Hotel
    • Sleep until you wake up naturally this morning Get ready for the afternoon tour.
    • After having lunch, go to Mogao Grottoes, which is an art palace combining ancient architecture, sculptures and murals, especially famous for its colorful murals. In the world, the richness and content of Dunhuang frescoes is unmatched by any religious grotto, temple or palace in the world today.
    • Ten in the evening, you will go to the wonder in the Mingsha Mountain Crescent Spring desert, free to move after arrival. You can enjoy the magical beauty of sand and springs, the sand does not cover the springs, and the springs are not exhausted. In addition, you can also experience desert activities such as sandboarding, camel riding, and desert karts.
    • After the tour, return to the downtown area of Dunhuang.
    Reminders:
    1. Before entering the desert area, it is recommended to protect valuables such as mobile phones to prevent sand from entering. At the same time, do not wear leather shoes that are easy to enter sand and dry the soles (in some hot weather), Bring sunglasses, sun hat, drinking water, etc.
    2. Go to Mingsha Mountain a little later in the afternoon, it will cause sunburn if it is too hot.It takes a long time to use the car in the morning. It is recommended that guests bring dry food (beef jerky + chocolate + mineral water + snickers, etc.)

  • Day 6 - Drive to Jiayuguan, Jiayuguan to Dunhuang.

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, drive to Jiayuguan, Jiayuguan to Dunhuang (approximately 380 kilometers, about 4.5 hours), you can enjoy the desert scenery along the way.
    • After arriving, you can take a break and enjoy lunch.
    • After having lunch, visit the westernmost dangerous pass of the world-famous Great Wall [Jiayuguan Gate Tower]. Jiayuguan Gate is located in the southwest corner of Jiayuguan Town on the Gobi Desert in Gansu Province. It is the western end of the Great Wall, the most complete and magnificent of the Great Wall. The pass, known as the "Tianxia Xiongguan" since ancient times, is the first pass to guard the Hexi Corridor, and it is also the place where the ancient Silk Road must pass, and then visit the Great Cantilever Wall.
    • In the evening, you are staying in Jiayuguan.
    Reminders:
    1. The one-way journey from Jiayuguan to Dunhuang is a long one today. It is recommended to bring dry food and water before departure, and take protection from sun protection.
    2. The Jiayuguan Great Wall does not allow photography, climbing, or scribbling

  • Day 7 - Drive to Zhangye City

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, drive to Zhangye City, the journey takes about 2.5 hours.
    • After arrival, visit Colorful Danxia, visit the most typical and largest Danxia landform in the arid area of our country Zhangye Colorful Danxia Landform (visit time is about 2 hours). The beauty of Danxia is a natural beauty without carving. During the day and throughout the year, there are different scenery for visitors to watch regardless of rain or shine. There is a hot air balloon experience program here. If tourists want to take a ride, please contact the consultant in advance to make an appointment.
    • Take a bus to Binggou Danxia. Compared with colorful Danxia, Binggou Danxia's landform is more majestic, more magical, and magnificent. Binggou Danxia was developed late and there are fewer tourists. You can walk around in the garden while thinking wildly.
    • Overnight staying in Zhangye.
    Reminders:
    1. . Zhangye Danxia landform has a relatively high temperature after noon. Considering everyone’s comfort, it is recommended to leave early in the morning and bring a coat with you to avoid catching cold.
    2. Due to weather factors, Hot air balloons need to be confirmed for the second time.

  • Day 8 - Departure by plane today

    • Return to the place of departure by plane today to end the pleasant trip to Qinghai and Gansu! Reminder: Pay attention to the time of taking your documents and belongings as well as the flight time.
