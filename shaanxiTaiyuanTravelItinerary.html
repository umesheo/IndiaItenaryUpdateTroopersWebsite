Taiyuan

About The Place

Taiyuan is the capital and largest city of Shanxi province, People's Republic of China. It is one of the main manufacturing bases of China. Throughout its long history, Taiyuan was the capital or provisional capital of many dynasties in China, hence the name Lóngchéng.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 : Arrival at Taiyuan and Explore Taiyuan

    Hotel
    • Arrive in Taiyuan today.
    • You will be picked up by our crew and you will be taken to the hotel for a rest by a car.
    • In the afternoon, the children will be taken into the China Coal Museum for a novel and simulated mine trip. Children will see different mining scenes from ancient times to the present, and understand the formation and hard-won of coal

  • Day 2 : Explore Taiyuan

    Hotel
    • After having breakfast, drive to Linfen, one of the birthplaces of the Chinese nation. During the trip, our guide will introduce you to Shanxi's local customs and culture.
    • In the afternoon, visit the Chinese annual rings, the local cultural heritage, the holy place of cultivation [Yunqiu Mountain Scenic Area], walk into the ancient village of Talpo, a living fossil of architecture, enjoy the characteristic folklore performance of Yunqiu Mountain, and participate in the making of flower buns.
    Warm Reminder:
    1. Yunqiu Mountain Scenic Area's policy closure time is from 2020.12.1 to 2021.2.9, and the park opening time is 2021.2.10 (New Year's Twenty-ninth), and Yunqiu Mountain Spring Festival Folklore Year will be held.

  • Day 3 : Explore Taiyuan

    Hotel
    • In the morning, walk into the Love Farm of the Food and Agriculture Education Center and learn about the cultivation of organic food under the leadership of the crop guide. Children can sow seeds and grow by themselves.
    • Then, enjoy the strongest sound of the Chinese Mother River [Huanghe Hukou Waterfall National Geological Park].
    • Afterwards, specially invited inheritors of Ansai waist drum in northern Shaanxi will lead the children and parents to learn to beat drums, play games, and sing folk songs.

  • Day 4 : Explore Taiyuan

    Hotel
    • In the morning, you will follow the popular Zhangbi Castle. Special arrangements will be made today. Everyone will go to the secret tunnel to play a mysterious treasure hunt.
    • Then you will make cloth tigers and experience intangible cultural heritage.
    • In the afternoon, you will visit Pingyao Ancient City accompanied by a guide. This is the financial center of China in the late Qing Dynasty. You can feel the atmosphere of Shanxi merchant culture everywhere.
    • Then you will watch the sitcom-"Seeing Pingyao Again". This drama made the culture of Shanxi merchants flourish. At the same time, it also showed that the new generation of us must inherit the achievements of our ancestors.

  • Day 5 : Explore Taiyuan

    Hotel
    • Today you will learn about the history and culture of the shopkeepers, as well as the financial territory of Shanxi merchants. The spirit of culture is heavy!
    • In the afternoon, go to Changjia Manor to learn about Wanli Tea Trade Road, and then the children will come to Old Vinegar Workshop to see how the ancient hand-made "Shanxi vinegar" is brewed, and make a peanut soaked in vinegar.

  • Day 6 - Explore Taiyuan

    • Go to Shanxi Museum in the morning with a guide, where you can see the essence of Shanxi's cultural relics, such as the Houma League book in the Spring and Autumn Period, spanning from ancient times the remains of ancient humans, until the history of Shanxi merchants during the Ming and Qing Dynasties.
    • At noon, come to the representative Shanxi Guild Hall, this is a veritable "noodle museum", and you can also watch the Shanxi noodle show.
    • The children will be taken to an authentic apprenticeship and learn how to make noodles with the master of noodle sculpture.
    • In the afternoon, visit the earliest extant royal garden Jinci in China. There are dozens of ancient buildings in the temple, which are very characteristic of Chinese traditional culture. Among them, Jinci's "Three Uniques" and "Three Treasures" are representative signs.

  • Day 7 - Return home

    • The exclusive personnel of our crew will arrange to drop off the plane according to the flight time.
    • You will return to your warm home.
